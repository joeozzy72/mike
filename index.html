Welcome to Michael's Adventure!

Michael wakes up in a strange land... glowing mushrooms, floating islands, and a map in his pocket. What does he do?

Following the Map

Michael follows the map and stumbles across a shimmering cave with ancient runes. A riddle is etched above the entrance.

"Only the one who knows their heart may enter."

Exploring the Forest

He meets a talking owl who offers to grant him one wish—but warns that all wishes come with a price.

The Cave of Truth

The door opens, and Michael walks into a realm of memories. He must choose one moment to relive and change.

The Owl's Bargain

Michael wishes for wisdom. In an instant, his hair turns silver and he understands the language of the stars.

Ending: Rewrite

Michael changes a single moment—and wakes up in a new timeline. Different, yet familiar. A second chance begins.

Ending: Peace

He walks out of the cave with clarity. The weight is gone. The world seems brighter, and he is finally free.

Ending: The Sage

Michael becomes a guide to travelers who lose their way. The owl watches from above, proud of his choice.
